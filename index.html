Breezy Acres Garden Center

Welcome to Breezy Acres Garden Center, where we take pride in providing the highest quality plants to our local community. As a family-owned business located in Auburn, New York, we have been dedicated to serving our customers for over 30 years. Our passion for plants runs deep; we believe plants can transform any space into a beautiful and serene oasis. Our family is here to help you find the perfect plants for your home or garden, and we can't wait to share our knowledge and enthusiasm with you!

Hanging Flowers

If you're looking for hanging plants for your porch or patio, Breezy Acres Garden Center is the perfect place to find them. Our selection of hanging plants includes ferns, ivies, and spider plants, ideal for creating a relaxing and beautiful atmosphere in your outdoor space or porch. Our team is here to guide you in choosing the perfect plants for your hanging planters and to provide you with all the information you need to care for them properly.

Potted Flowers

Breezy Acres Garden Center offers an impressive variety of potted flowers to brighten any room or outdoor space. Our potted flowers are of the highest quality, and we take great care to ensure they are healthy and vibrant. Our knowledgeable team is always on hand to help you choose the perfect potted flowers for your needs, and we have a wide variety of pots and planters to choose from so you can find the ideal match for your style and preferences.

Garden Plants

At Breezy Acres Garden Center, we are committed to helping you create a thriving and beautiful garden. We offer a wide variety of garden plants, including vegetables, herbs, and flowers, and our team is always available to provide expert advice on how to care for them. We believe gardening is a beautiful way to relax in nature and enjoy the outdoors, and we are excited to share our love of gardening with you. Whether you're a seasoned gardener or just starting, we have the plants and expertise to create a stunning and flourishing garden.
