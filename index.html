Breezy Acres Garden Center

Welcome to Breezy Acres Garden Center, where we take pride in providing the highest quality plants to our local community. As a family-owned business located in Auburn, New York, we have been dedicated to serving our customers for over 30 years. Our passion for plants runs deep; we believe plants can transform any space into a beautiful and serene oasis. Our family is here to help you find the perfect plants for your home or garden, and we can't wait to share our knowledge and enthusiasm with you. !
